Specializing in a user-focused approach, bold ideas, and playful interfaces.

As a designer, I like to stay attentive to the details and intricacies of each project, ensuring that the final product is well thought out and retains its value over time.

My vision as a designer is deeply rooted in fully owning the materials I work with. To me, a design is only truly successful when every element has been created, adapted, or improved by me to perfectly align with my vision and desires. This process of refining and perfecting not only brings me greater satisfaction, but also helps me to truly make my work my own.

For me, good design is all about individuality—the ability to make a design instantly recognizable as your unique creation. That, in my eyes, is what sets a designer apart from the crowd. This doesn’t just mean that a design should be visually striking, but also that it tells a story that reflects my perspective and values.

Ultimately, I see design as an expression of my individuality—a way to share my vision of the world and create something lasting and meaningful for myself and those around me.

Beyond my work as a design student, you’ll often find me behind the decks, spinning obscure tunes and digging through record store crates to uncover hidden gems. When I’m not immersed in music, I’m usually honing my creativity by experimenting with graphic design, producing music, or exploring new projects in TouchDesigner.

In my free time, I enjoy making cocktails, watching films, and escaping into different worlds through both fantasy novels and classic literature.
