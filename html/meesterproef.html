If it ain’t broke Don’t Fix It. – Meesterproef Visual Interface Design

An interactive retelling of a Follow The Money article covering the use of an outdated mathematical model utilised by Dutch Airports to calculate safety zones around runways. The content of this article was visualised as an interactive and highly stylised one-pager.

The design for the aviation case was guided by a balance between aesthetics and visual impact. The subject—outdated digital infrastructure in aviation—is both technical and complex, yet it directly affects safety and trust. My goal was to create an interface that is both informative and engaging, highlighting the urgency and politics of modernization.

To communicate this, I used pixelated visuals and retro-inspired elements to emphasize the age of the current computational models. At the same time, animated transitions and interactive components invite exploration and discovery. Through these visuals and interactions, I aimed to add dynamism and tangibility to otherwise abstract and complex information, making the topic more accessible and thought-provoking for users.
