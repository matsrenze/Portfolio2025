Dieter Räms inspired - Digital Record Player

A working and heavily stylized music player made in HTMl/CSS and Javascript, with a specific focus on providing accessibility for users reliant on screen readers and voice command based interaction.

With a Design based on a handheld 45” record player originally devised and made product by the great Dieter Rams.

Progress

To create a suitable product I was tasked with rapidly improving my programming abilities to create a a functioning product that fulfilled its intended purpose, while also being visually appealing, Responsive and accessible.
